About ScoutConnect

ScoutConnect is a platform designed to bridge the gap between professional sports scouts and grassroots talent. We believe that exceptional athletes can emerge from anywhere, and our mission is to ensure they get the visibility they deserve.

For Athletes

Create a profile showcasing your skills, achievements, and performance statistics. Upload videos, track your progress, and get discovered by professional scouts from clubs, national teams, and sports agencies worldwide.

For Scouts

Access a diverse pool of talented athletes from grassroots communities. Filter by sport, location, age group, and performance metrics. Connect directly with potential prospects and discover the next generation of sports stars.
